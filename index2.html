How to Get Alerts/Warnings By Agencies Now follow This Steps

Enable Notifications

1. Go To Home Screen

2. Hold Press to YoloWeather App

3. Enable The Notification if it's disabled by default

4. You can Customize Notification Sound of YoloWeather App For understanding sound alert but sometimes Notification sound also played by Agencies so you can change or not your choice

Keep Autostart Background App YoloWeather

1. Go to Settings

2. And Click Apps/AppManage In Android

3. Search YoloWeather and Click The App and Click the App Battery Usage Then Click again and set to unrestrict
